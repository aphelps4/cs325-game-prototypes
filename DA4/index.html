Master Crafter

  • Originality: Players can craft and place items on the ground.
  • Technical Merit:
    • Opening and closing menu
    • Function that shows clickable objects
    • Placeable objects
    • Limited resources
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned how to make generic objects so that I can rely on called variables or functions for different objects. I have to be careful with all my menus because they can easily break each other.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Font: Cooper Black
    • Images and sprites: self-made through paint.net
    • Music: self-made through Caustic and Audacity
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Stardew Valley
  • High Concept: A carpenter finds the surrounding area to be too empty and decides to decorate it.
  • Theme: Carpenter and chicken.
  • Mandated Variety:
    • Input: direct, keyboard, mouse
    • Randomness: deterministic
    • Play styles: Artist, Collector
    • Genres: Overhead
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is evaluating the feel of picking and placing objects.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want players to decorate the area around them to their liking.
  • Gameplay: Players can move around the area by using the arrow keys. Players can use the mouse to select menu options, select items from their inventory, and to place items.
  • Strategies: Players will place their objects to get a feel for what they like the looks of.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: A carpenter wants to decorate their surrounding area. They use their skills to collect resources and craft the furniture they need to place around.
  • Target Audience: This game will be targeted at a casual audience who enjoys decorating areas.
  • Play Time: 1 minute.
