Cat Cafe

  • Originality: Once you launch your cats, you no longer have any control over where they land.
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
    • Using the shared object created in main for shared functions.
    • Made cats move based on angle shot from cannon. (Possibly want to make them shoot based on mouse distance from cannon as well)
    • Beds are placed randomly in four sections of the area.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I finally learned to put functions in the shared object in main instead of just copy-pasting them in every state that uses them. I also tried to make the functions generic so they are easily useable for any needs I may have from them. Unfortunately, I wasted a lot of time trying to figure out how to rotate a body just to find out that I can not access the physics type that would let me do that.

  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Angry Birds
  • High Concept: The player must use their mouse to aim and shoot their cats into the beds spread around the cafe in order to turn their cafe into a cat cafe.
  • Theme: Person with lots and lots of cats and celebrity sighting.
  • Mandated Variety:
    • Input: indirect, mouse
    • Randomness: random
    • Play styles: Achiever, Joker
    • Genres: Shooter
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is evaluating launching objects in an area with gravity and the feel of that play style.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should be able to pick up this game easily but still have a fun time playing it.
  • Gameplay: Move the mouse to aim the cannon and click to shoot cats into cat beds.
  • Strategies: Players can shoot towards beds that are closer to the cannon to safely get a feel of the physics in this game.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: A person with a lot of cats heard that their favorite celebrity was sighted in the area. They devise a plan to open a cat cafe with all their cats in an attempt to attract the celebrity because they heard that cat cafes were trendy. The player quickly sets up their cafe by shooting the cats into the beds spread around the area.
  • Target Audience: This game will be targeted at a more casual audience of all ages.
  • Play Time: 5 minutes.
