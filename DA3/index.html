  • Theme: Person with lots and lots of cats and celebrity sighting.
  • Mandated Variety: State which input, randomness, genre, and play style you used in this prototype. input: mouse

  • Player Experience Goals: Players should be able to pick up this game easily but still have a fun time playing it.
  • Gameplay: Move the mouse to aim the cannon and click to shoot cats into cat beds.
  • Strategies: Players can shoot towards beds that are closer to the cannon to safely get a feel of the physics in this game.

A person with a lot of cats heard that their favorite celebrity was sighted in the area. They devise a plan to open a cat cafe with all their cats in an attempt to attract the celebrity because they heard that cat cafes were trendy. The player quickly sets up their cafe by shooting the cats into the beds spread around the area.
